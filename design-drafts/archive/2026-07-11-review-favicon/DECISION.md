# Decision: Review Favicon

## Final Direction

Use a dark rounded-square favicon containing a white document outline and a warm-gray comment bubble.

## Why

The mark directly represents Markdown review, stays recognizable at favicon size, and fits the quiet monochrome Codex interface better than the generic blue globe.

## Superseded

- Generic browser globe: did not identify the product.
- Blue-accent concepts: visually disconnected from the selected Codex-native direction.
- Quote, diff, and stacked-page concepts: less immediately recognizable as document comments.

## Landed In

- `public/favicon.svg`
- `public/favicon-32.png`
- `public/apple-touch-icon.png`
- `index.html`

## Baseline Updated

No. The favicon is outside the captured in-app runtime surface and does not change its DOM layout or component styling.
